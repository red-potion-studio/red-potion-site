Welcome to the pages of the Dark Guide to Everything!

This volume compiles game content, rules, and inspirations designed for dark fantasy campaigns and adventures, or for infusing a macabre and sinister tone wherever needed.

Structured in the style of the Guides to Everything, this handbook aims to provide a diverse range of material, ensuring you’ll always find something useful for a variety of situations.

Each piece of content is also enriched with lore and detailed descriptions, offering narrative hooks to seamlessly integrate it into your story.
