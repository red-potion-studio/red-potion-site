Our Mission

At Red Potion Studio, we bring tabletop worlds to life through visual design, digital adaptation, and storytelling.

We specialize in crafting immersive manuals, Roll20/Fantasy Grounds/Foundry modules, and promotional media that capture the essence of your game and connect it to players worldwide.

Our mission is to empower creators and publishers in the TTRPG industry with beautiful, functional, and narrative-driven assets—so your stories don’t just play, they shine.

Graphic & Layout

We create manuals, supplements, and digital books that are both beautiful and easy to navigate. Every layout is crafted to enhance readability and immerse players in the world you’ve built.

VTT Conversion

We adapt your adventures and resources for Roll20, Fantasy Grounds Unity, and FoundryVTT, ensuring maps, tokens, and modules are fully interactive, functional, and ready for smooth gameplay online.

Content Design

From rich lore and compelling questlines to balanced mechanics and content writing, we help you develop narratives and game systems that captivate players and bring your tabletop worlds to life.

Marketing Assets

We produce animations, banners, social graphics, and other promotional materials that reflect your game’s identity, helping you engage your audience and showcase your content effectively.

DMsGuild Partnership

As officially Vetted Graphic Designers for the Dungeon Masters Guild, we specialize in producing publication-ready manuals that meet Print on Demand standards.

We offer professional layout and graphic design tailored for OneBookShelf’s publishing requirements, ensuring that your books are visually striking and fully compliant with DMsGuild print specifications.

  • • Print-on-Demand layout compliant with DMsGuild guidelines
  • • Production manuals following the D&D layout, both 2014 and 2024 editions
  • • Flexible payment options — including royalty share agreements when suitable

If you’re preparing a release for the DMsGuild, we can help you turn your manuscript into a polished, print-ready* product.

*Print-ready products must comply with the requirements detailed here, but with us it is also possible to produce a fully digital-only manual.

Roll20 Partnership

We develop Roll20 Addons and Compendium modules designed to make your content playable and visually cohesive online.

From map setup and journal linking to token automation and compendium entries, we transform your book’s content into a fully integrated virtual experience for GMs and players alike.

  • • Compendium-ready formatting and data structure
  • • Custom graphic design and layout for Handouts and Compendium pages
  • • Token, Tables, and Handout integration
  • • Module packaging ready for Roll20 Marketplace

Ideal for publishers expanding to digital play or creators releasing their first VTT module.
